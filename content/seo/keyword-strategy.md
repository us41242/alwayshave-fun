# alwayshave.fun — Keyword Strategy
_Last updated: 2026-04-04_

## Personas & Target Queries

---

### 1. The Weekend Warrior
**Profile:** Safety-first, dog dad/mom, family trips, beginner-to-moderate hiker. Wants to know it's safe before leaving the driveway.

**Short-tail (rankable now):**
- dog-friendly hikes nevada
- easy hiking trails utah
- family hiking trails arizona
- hiking weather forecast
- safe hiking this weekend

**Long-tail (high intent, low competition):**
- best dog-friendly hikes near las vegas
- easy hiking trails with water for dogs utah
- safe family hiking trails arizona current air quality
- kid-friendly hikes colorado weather conditions today
- hiking trails utah open today no permit required
- [trail name] safe for dogs today
- best weekend hiking trips with family nevada
- hiking trails current air quality [state]

**Content angle:** Always answer "is it safe today?" — score + AQI + temp in plain English. Dog-friendly callout on every trail page.

---

### 2. The Hardcore Overlander
**Profile:** 4WD, high-clearance, remote access, technical terrain data. Doesn't care about scenic photos — cares about road conditions and permit windows.

**Short-tail:**
- 4WD trails nevada
- off-road trail conditions utah
- jeep trails arizona
- high clearance trails southwest
- remote camping trails

**Long-tail:**
- technical 4WD trails nevada current conditions
- high clearance vehicle trails utah road conditions
- off-road trail difficulty ratings gps coordinates
- arizona remote overlanding trails fire road access
- jeep trails utah water crossing conditions
- backcountry trail access permit requirements [state]
- [trail name] road conditions high clearance required
- overlanding route planning southwest terrain difficulty

**Content angle:** Lead with road access, vehicle requirements, permit windows. Link to official alerts. Score system for road + weather + fire risk.

---

### 3. The Nature Photographer
**Profile:** Chases golden hour, needs clear skies + low AQI. Niche audience but extremely high intent — travels specifically for conditions.

**Short-tail:**
- golden hour hiking trails
- best visibility hikes utah
- sunrise trails nevada
- low AQI hiking

**Long-tail:**
- hiking trails utah best visibility clear air quality
- golden hour photography locations nevada trails
- hiking trails arizona low AQI dust-free conditions
- best sunrise sunset trails utah visibility today
- landscape photography hiking trails clear skies
- nevada hikes clear views haze forecast
- photography hikes visibility conditions this week
- [trail name] visibility AQI forecast

**Content angle:** AQI front and center. Forecast card. Best time of day callout. These users will share photos on Instagram — social signal opportunity.
